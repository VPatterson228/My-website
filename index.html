WTA Mission

The Washington Trails Association (WTA) serves as the advocate for hikers across Washington state. We safeguard hiking trails and wilderness areas, mobilize volunteers for trail maintenance, and advocate for hiking as an enjoyable and beneficial outdoor activity for all.

Voluteer Mission

Our community, which includes you if you're reading this, is the essence of the Washington Trails Association. Engage with us to support our efforts and contribute to the preservation of the trails you cherish. Whether you wish to join as a member, participate in trail maintenance, advocate for wilderness areas, or connect with fellow hikers, there's an opportunity awaiting you.
